Welcome to Falgiatore Construction L.L.C.- PA035736

Hello, my name is Tom Falgiatore. I am the owner/operator of Falgiatore Construction, L.L.C. and a life long resident of Chester County. I am a graduate of West Chester University with a BS in Marketing. I started in the construction industry in 1987. My company specializes in providing quality home improvements and repairs at reasonable rates. We can get the job done right for you, however big or small the project may be. We are fully insured, including worker's compensation and we are a licensed PA contractor. We also provide free estimates.

Here are some of the services we provide:

  • Finished basements and attics
  • Home offices
  • Kitchens and baths
  • Deck installation, repairs and maintenance
  • Custom painting
  • Tile installation
  • Finish trim, wainscoting and crown moldings
  • Doors and windows
  • Drywall installation and repairs

So if you are looking for a reliable contact in the home improvement industry, please give us a call and join our other satisfied customers.
